General Service Guidelines
  • Inspect all O-rings during each service; replace if worn or cracked.
  • Use manufacturer-recommended silicone grease on all moving parts.
  • Check spring tension on exhaust valves; replace springs every 2-3 years.
  • Clean threads with isopropyl alcohol before reassembly.
  • Do not over-tighten valves; hand-tight plus 1/4 turn is typically sufficient.
